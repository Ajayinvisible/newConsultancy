Introduction to the United Kingdom

The United Kingdom also known by the name Great Britain is an island Country in the West of Europe. It is surrounded by the water bodies of the Atlantic Ocean, North Sea, English Channel, and the Irish sea. The four states of the UK are England, Scotland, Northern Island, and Wales. The Capital City of the United Kingdom is London. The people of this Country are called the British. The religious beliefs of people are mainly Christianity. The head of the Monarchy of the United Kingdom is Queen Elizabeth second since 6 February 1952. The head of government of the United Kingdom is led by Prime Minister Mr. Boris Johnson.

Study in the UK:

The education system of the United Kingdom is popularly known as the British Education System. The main parts of education in the UK are primary education, secondary education, further education, and higher education. Some of the popular further education qualifications are A-levels, GNVQs, Btechs and higher education degrees are Foundation courses, Bachelors, Masters, and Doctorate degrees. The most popular world-class Universities for advanced education in the UK are the University of Oxford, the University of Cambridge, The University of Edinburgh, the University of Warwick, the University of Manchester, the University of Warwick, the University of London, and the University of Glasgow. The most popular colleges and top ranking in the World are Imperial College London, London School of Economics, KingsCollege, etc.
